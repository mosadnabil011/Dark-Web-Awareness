Glossary

  • Dark Web: A hidden part of the internet accessible only through special tools like Tor.
  • Tor Browser: A browser that allows access to the Dark Web while ensuring anonymity.
  • VPN (Virtual Private Network): A tool that encrypts your internet connection to protect your identity.
  • Phishing: Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information through deceptive emails or links.
  • Cryptocurrency: Digital currency like Bitcoin, often used for anonymous transactions.
  • Deep Web: Parts of the internet not indexed by search engines but distinct from the Dark Web.
  • Malware: Malicious software designed to harm or exploit devices.
