FAQs

  1. Q: Is accessing the Dark Web illegal?

    A: No, accessing the Dark Web is not illegal. However, engaging in unlawful activities is against the law.

  2. Q: Do I need special tools to access the Dark Web?

    A: Yes, you need tools like the Tor Browser and a VPN for safe access.

  3. Q: Can I be tracked while using the Dark Web?

    A: While tools like Tor provide anonymity, nothing is completely untraceable. Use additional precautions like a VPN.

  4. Q: What should I avoid on the Dark Web?

    A: Avoid illegal marketplaces, scams, and sharing personal information.
