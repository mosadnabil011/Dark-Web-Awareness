Advantages

  • Provides anonymity for activists, journalists, and whistleblowers.
  • Enables access to uncensored content in restrictive regimes.
  • Supports private transactions using cryptocurrencies.
  • Freedom of speech in oppressive regimes.
  • Access to valuable resources and whistleblowing platforms.


Disadvantages

  • Facilitates illegal activities like drug trafficking and hacking.
  • Increases risks of scams, malware, and phishing attacks.
  • Difficult to monitor or regulate effectively.
  • Illegal activities and harmful content.
  • Difficulty in verifying the legitimacy of services.
  • Potential exposure to scams and malware.
