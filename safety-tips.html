Safety Tips

Staying safe on the Dark Web requires vigilance and preparation:

    1- Always use a VPN to encrypt your internet traffic.
    2- Access the Dark Web only through trusted browsers like Tor.
    3- Avoid sharing personal information or sensitive data.
    4- Be cautious about clicking unknown links or downloading files.
    5- Stick to legal activities and stay informed about the latest safety practices.
